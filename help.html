FAQs

Common Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time through our mobile app or by clicking the 'Track Order' link in your confirmation email.

What are the delivery fees?

Delivery fees vary based on your location. Most orders within a 5-mile radius have a flat fee of $2.99.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of being placed. After that, the kitchen starts preparing your meal.

Do you offer vegan options?

Yes! Check our 'Healthy Salads' and 'Fresh Pizzas' sections for vegan-friendly choices.
